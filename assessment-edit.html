How to generate your exam JSON

Follow these steps to convert your exam document into the format this platform expects.

  1. 01 Open claude.ai in a new tab and start a fresh conversation.
  2. 02 Attach your exam document (PDF or DOCX) to the message. Include your answers either in a separate file or directly within the exam document (e.g., the correct option highlighted or in bold).
  3. 03 Copy the prompt below and paste it into the same message, then send.
  4. 04 Claude will reply with a JSON file. Download the file and save it on your computer.
  5. 05 Come back here and upload the .json file. Review the questions, upload any required images, then save.
